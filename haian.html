Overview of Hai’an City

Nestled in Nantong, Jiangsu Province, Hai’an stands as a pivotal industrial city within the Yangtze River Delta economic circle, boasting decades of deep manufacturing heritage, a complete industrial supply chain, and mature cross-border export logistics.

Geographically well-located, it connects core Chinese manufacturing zones with global trade routes, making it a trusted one-stop sourcing base for overseas buyers across multiple sectors including power equipment, new energy, precision machinery and textile.

Textile & Light Manufacturing

Unmatched Logistics & Export Advantages

As a national comprehensive multi-modal logistics hub, Hai’an integrates four convenient international shipping channels:

  • Highway network linking Shanghai, Nanjing and all coastal ports
  • High-speed rail for fast sample & business travel
  • Inland river transport to Shanghai & Nantong Port for sea consolidation
  • Stable China-Europe freight trains direct to European markets

Sea-rail combined transport cuts logistics cost & shortens lead time for bulk industrial orders. Local streamlined customs clearance supports smooth global shipment of power equipment and machinery.
