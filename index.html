Strategic Location & Premium Export Logistics Support

Located at the core node of the Yangtze River Delta, Hai'an is a top-tier industrial and export hub in central Jiangsu. It boasts a fully covered transportation network including high-speed railways, expressways, inland waterways, and bonded logistics centers.

With regular China-Europe freight trains, low-cost waterway shipping for oversized power equipment, and 1.5-hour access to major international airports, Hai’an provides unmatched, cost-effective and reliable global shipping solutions for all power and new energy equipment exports.

Complete Power & New Energy Industrial Ecosystem (Full-Capability Support)

Hai'an is a nationally recognized professional manufacturing base for power equipment and new energy electrical systems, featuring a complete upstream and downstream supply chain, mature industrial supporting facilities, and large-scale standardized production capacity.

Beyond electrical industry advantages, Hai’an owns solid foundation in heavy machinery, precision processing, new materials and advanced manufacturing sectors, including special glass, CNC pipe bending equipment and mining machinery. This diversified industrial ecosystem provides stronger structural support for our heavy-duty processing, customized manufacturing and cross-industry project matching capability.

Empowered by this robust industrial cluster, we delivers diversified, integrated electrical equipment and systematic solutions to meet the full-scenario needs of global clients, covering mature core products and extended new energy supporting series:

1. Mature Core Products (Stable & Verified Supply)

We specialize in core power distribution equipment, including conventional power transformers, kiosk substations, high and low voltage switchgears, as well as supporting insulated magnetic materials and electrical accessories. All products support personalized customization, mass standardized production, and full-process delivery for global power projects, with stable quality and proven project experience.

2. New Energy Extended Product Series (Market-Oriented Supporting Solutions)

Leveraging Hai'an’s sound new energy industrial foundation and complete supporting resources, we offer professional supporting equipment tailored to the booming global renewable energy market.

Our product portfolio covers PV and energy storage dedicated transformers, wind power supporting electrical equipment, intelligent power distribution systems, and turnkey solutions for energy storage stations, fully adapting to the technical standards and project requirements of global new energy projects.

3. Customized Heavy-Duty Electrical Equipment & Project Supporting Services

Backed by the local advanced heavy manufacturing and precision processing supply chain, we provide customized heavy-duty electrical equipment and supporting processing services for large infrastructure engineering, new energy station construction, and industrial intelligent equipment projects, meeting the personalized and high-standard manufacturing demands of complex overseas projects.

Cooperation Value

As a core export manufacturing hub in central Jiangsu, Hai'an is equipped with national economic development zones and international industrial parks, with standardized export procedures and complete international trade service systems.

Local power equipment manufacturers possess rich experience in overseas EPC projects and large-scale equipment export, maintaining long-term stable business cooperation with clients in Southeast Asia, North America, South America, Central Asia and other Belt and Road regions.

Unlike manufacturers with single product lines, we rely on Hai'an's integrated industrial chain advantages to provide one-stop, full-series electrical equipment supporting services. This model effectively reduces clients’ procurement costs, unifies quality standards, shortens project delivery cycles, and supports long-term strategic cooperation, framework orders and customized project development for global partners.
