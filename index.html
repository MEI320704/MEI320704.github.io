City Profile

Hai'an sits at the junction of Nantong, Yancheng and Taizhou, bordering the Yellow Sea and Yangtze River, serving as a provincial transport hub and core node of Yangtze River Delta.

Hai'an bears nine national honorary titles, including Hometown of Equipment Manufacturing, Silk & Textile, Construction & Education. It has been awarded National Civilized City, National Sanitary City and National Ecological Model City.

Equipped with national economic development zone, provincial high-tech zone, Sino-Italy Industrial Park and multiple logistics parks, Hai'an maintains steady foreign trade growth and becomes a core export manufacturing base in central Jiangsu.

Transportation Advantages

  • Railway & Highway: Full coverage of high-speed rail & expressways, 1-hour access to Shanghai metropolitan area. Railway bonded logistics center with regular China-Europe freight trains for heavy machinery delivery.
  • Water Transport: Close to Nantong Port & Shanghai Port, inland waterways run across the city, offering low-cost shipping for large power transformers & electrical equipment.
  • Air Travel: 1.5-hour drive to 3 major airports (Nantong, Shanghai Hongqiao, Pudong) for global business trips.

Core Industrial Clusters

Adhering to "Industry-driven City & Advanced Manufacturing" strategy, Hai'an boasts complete industrial chains covering power equipment and diversified heavy manufacturing sectors:

  • High-end Equipment Manufacturing: National base for elevator parts, forging & magnetic materials. Mature industrial cluster for power transformers, kiosk substations, high & low voltage switchgears with full upstream & downstream supporting suppliers.
  • New Energy: Rapid growth of PV, energy storage & wind power equipment, with strong market demand for solar and energy storage transformers.
  • Advanced Textile: World-class integrated silk industrial chain with fabrics exported worldwide.
  • New Materials & Electronic Information: Local suppliers of magnetic insulation materials and semiconductor accessories for electrical power equipment.
  • Special Glass Manufacturing: Major domestic production base for functional special glass, automotive coated glass and high-end architectural glass. Custom deep-processing services are available to supply new energy vehicles, construction and intelligent equipment industries.
  • CNC Pipe Bending Equipment: Dedicated cluster for numerical control pipe benders and precision metal forming machinery. Fully supported supply chain widely adopted in power engineering, construction machinery and pipeline projects.
  • Mining Heavy Machinery: Complete manufacturing chain for intelligent mining equipment and spare parts. High-reliability heavy machinery products are supplied for mineral exploitation and large-scale infrastructure projects.

Business & Living Environment

Eco-livable City: Dense river networks, waterfront parks and multiple A-level scenic spots.

Cooperation Value

Benefit from Hai'an complete power manufacturing supply chain and global logistics network. It is the preferred cooperation city for overseas power equipment purchasers and export-oriented manufacturers.
