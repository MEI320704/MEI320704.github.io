Company Profile

Jiangsu ACME POWER CO.,LTD. is located in Hai'an High-Tech Industrial Zone, Nantong, Jiangsu Province, specializing in electrical power industry for more than 15 years. We are an integrated enterprise focusing on R&D, manufacturing and global export of main transformers, distribution transformers, 110kV/220kV ultra-high voltage transformers, dry-type transformers, oil-immersed transformers, pre-installed substations, various box transformers and high voltage switchgears.

Our factory covers an area of 200,000 square meters, with more than 150 engineering and technical personnel and 800 employees. Benefiting from the complete supporting supply chain of power equipment in Hai'an, our factory is equipped with standardized production workshops, VPD & VPL vacuum drying production lines and full set of type test equipment. All products are manufactured strictly in accordance with international standards including IEEE, UL, ANSI, IEC, NEMA and DOE. Our factory has obtained a full set of authoritative certifications including UL certification, ISO9001 Quality Management System, ISO14001 Environmental Management System and KEMA certification, fully adapting to power grid operation conditions in North America, Europe, Middle East, Latin America and Southeast Asia.

Our core product portfolio includes main transformers, distribution transformers, pole-mounted distribution transformers, pad-mount transformers, 33kV outdoor kiosk substations, 110kV & 220kV ultra-high voltage transformers, pre-installed box substations, high voltage switchgears, and 1000VAC dry-type isolation transformers for energy storage. Customization services are available, including IEEE 693 seismic reinforcement design and sea transport shock protection design.

We mainly focus on overseas markets, with products exported to USA, Canada, Mexico, European countries, Middle East and Philippines. We have a professional overseas engineering team providing one-stop services including drawing customization, on-site technical coordination and long-term after-sales maintenance. We attach great importance to on-time delivery and product stability, achieving an overseas reorder rate over 65%.

Corporate Mission: To make power transmission more efficient, safer and smarter. We focus on the layout of the entire transformer industry chain, integrate cutting-edge technologies and innovative processes, and provide global customers with one-stop power solutions from R&D and design to after-sales operation and maintenance. Choosing ACME POWER means choosing professionalism, quality and trust. We look forward to working with you hand in hand to create a better future!
